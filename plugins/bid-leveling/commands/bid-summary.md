# Bid Summary Command

Provide a quick high-level comparison of uploaded contractor bids.

## Time Estimate

Show this at the start:
```
ESTIMATED TIME: ~2-3 minutes total
- Data Extraction: ~1 minute
- Comparison: ~1-2 minutes
- CURRENT PHASE: Starting
```

## What to do

1. Read bid documents using available runtime (Node.js preferred on Windows).
2. Provide the amount of time it took for step 1 to complete and a new estimate to complete the remaining steps.
3. If PDFs are present and pdf-converter MCP is available, convert to Excel first.
4. Provide the amount of time it took for steps 1, 2, and 3 to complete and a new estimate to complete the remaining steps.
5. Produce a quick comparison table: bidder name, total amount, key exclusions, notable allowances, red flags.
6. Provide the amount of time it took for steps 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 to complete and a new estimate to complete the remaining steps.
7. Flag what a full leveling analysis would uncover.
8. Provide the amount of time it took for steps 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7 to complete and a new estimate to complete the remaining steps.
9. Recommend /level-bids for complete analysis or /level-bids-auto for fast no-questions analysis.
